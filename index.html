About

Iowa Girls Code Camp is the first ever coding camp for high school girls in the state of Iowa. While Computer Science is one of the fastest growing majors today, women make up only 18% of graduates.

Our goal is to educate young Iowa women about careers in Computer Science and give them the skills to start coding on their own.

Is it free to attend?

Yes, the event is totally free to attend, and includes breakfast and lunch.
Simply register here.

Do I need prior coding experience?

Nope! This event is geared toward total beginners, to show how easy it is to get started coding.
